Our Model

How We Work

From your idea to a live AI-powered platform — in three phases

01

Share Your Vision

Tell us about your content, audience, and brand. We map out the platform architecture that fits your goals — CourseBase, Smart Library, or the Full System.

02

We Build & Customize

Our team builds your branded platform with your domain, content, and identity. The AI assistant is trained exclusively on your materials using RAG — you review, we refine.

03

Launch & Own

Your platform goes live under your brand. You own everything — the code, the data, the revenue. We provide documentation and optional ongoing support.

04

Grow Your Business

Add courses, expand content, serve more students. Your AI assistant keeps learning from your materials — your platform grows with you.

Privacy by design: Every platform we build processes data within the client's own infrastructure. AI assistants are trained using RAG — no data is sent to external models or shared with third parties.
